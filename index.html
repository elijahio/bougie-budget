What is Bougie Budget?


Bougie Budget is a tool that helps you calculate your future savings (or losses) based on your levels of income and expenses. This tool also helps you compare your spending with average spending in specific categories, like rent and utilities, pulled specifically from your area—based on the ZIP code you entered upon arriving on the site.
